Critical Lens Analysis

This project explores Starr’s adolescent experience and resilience through a Critical Race lens. It highlights how trauma, identity, and systemic inequities shape her growth and activism. The website uses original photography and design to visualize her journey and key themes of hope, justice, and community support.
